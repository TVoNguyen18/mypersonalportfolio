A little about me

What I think about coding

I think that the future lies in the hands of the tech industry. As technology evolves, our machines take over our jobs. As long as robots are not smart enough to evade our grasp, we can control them using code. That is why I believe that schools should make room in their curriculum to teach students coding.

What I love about reading

When I've gone through an exhausting day, I like to curl up and read. Reading is a real stress reliever. You can lose yourself in the world of your book and forgot that your mom had told you to fold the laundry. Sorry, Mom.

Here are my hobbies:
